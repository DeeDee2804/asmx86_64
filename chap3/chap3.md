**8/ Provide the decimal representation of the following IEEE 32 bit floating point values.**
- **0xC1440000** = -12.25
- **0x41440000** = 12.25
- **0xC0D00000** = 11000000110100000000000000000000<sub>2</sub>
                 = - 1.101 x 2<sup>2</sup>
